Who is Daniel Reisinger?
Daniel Reisinger finished his doctoral studies at the University of Graz, where he specialized in complexity science and computational modeling. His work on critical transitions, early warning signals, and complex contagions has important implications for various fields such as ecology, sociology, and physics.

Born: November 11, 1993, in Vöcklabruck, Austria
Education: Doctor of Philosophy, at the University of Graz
Current Affiliation: Institute of Biology
Previous Affiliation: Institute of Environmental Systems Sciences
Research Interests: Critical transitions, Early warning signals, Complex contagions, Systems science, Network science, Swarm intelligent systems
